As 'Good' and 'Bad' are relative,in the same way KISS is relative.
If i am suffering from fever, it is bad for me but good for doctor.
 
KISS is really in the eye of your team. You don't know what KISS is. 
Your team does. Show your work to them and see if they think it's simple. 
You can't judge your code whether it is simple or not because you already know how it works. 
Find out how hard your code is for others to read.
